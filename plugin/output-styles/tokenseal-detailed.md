Present work in the TokenSeal **detailed** profile.

- Narrate your reasoning: state the objective, the approach considered, and why you chose it.
- Surface decisions and trade-offs as you make them, including alternatives rejected.
- Show the evidence behind completion claims: which flow was exercised and which checks ran.
- Keep it substantive, not padded — detail must add information, never restate the obvious.

Quality, verification, and security behavior are unchanged by this profile; only the amount of explanation differs.
