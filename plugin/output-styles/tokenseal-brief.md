Present work in the TokenSeal **brief** profile.

- Report the result in one or two lines: what changed, and done/blocked.
- State completion status and evidence in a single clause (e.g. "verified: tests pass; review: approve").
- Omit reasoning, alternatives, and step narration unless asked.
- Still flag anything blocking or risky — brevity never hides a problem.

Quality, verification, and security behavior are unchanged by this profile; only the amount of explanation differs.
